🔑 Step 1: Generate a Temporary Access Pass (TAP)

A TAP is a time-limited, single-use code that lets you sign in to Microsoft services and bootstrap your FIDO2 passkey registration.

—

🛡️ Step 2: Register Your Passkey at aka.ms/mysecurityinfo

Microsoft requires passkey registration to happen through their official security info page. Use your TAP to sign in, then add your passkey.

  1. 1
    Open a new tab and go to aka.ms/mysecurityinfo
  2. 2
    When prompted to sign in, enter your work email and select "Use a Temporary Access Pass"
  3. 3
    Enter the TAP code shown above
  4. 4
    On the Security info page, click "Add sign-in method" → select "Passkey (FIDO2)"
  5. 5
    Follow the prompts to register your security key or device passkey. Name it something recognisable (e.g. "YubiKey 5", "MacBook Touch ID")
  6. 6
    Once registered, your passkey is ready. You can now sign in to any Microsoft service without a password ✅

🧰 What You'll Need

  • •
    A FIDO2 security key (e.g. YubiKey, Google Titan) or a device that supports passkeys (Windows Hello, Touch ID, Face ID)
  • •
    Your Microsoft work account — you're already signed in above
  • •
    A few minutes in a private, secure location (don't register on a public computer)

📋 Step-by-Step Registration Instructions

  1. 1
    Click "Go to Security Info" below. Microsoft's Security Info page will open in a new tab.
  2. 2
    If prompted to sign in again, use your work email () and your existing password or MFA method.
  3. 3
    On the Security Info page, click "Add sign-in method".
  4. 4
    From the dropdown, select "Passkey (FIDO2)" and click "Add".
  5. 5
    Hardware security key: Insert into a USB port (or hold near your device for NFC), then touch the gold disc or button on the key when prompted.
  6. 6
    Device passkey (Windows Hello / Touch ID / Face ID): Follow the on-screen biometric or PIN prompt on your device.
  7. 7
    Give your passkey a recognisable name (e.g. "YubiKey 5 – Work", "MacBook Touch ID") and click "Done".
  8. 8
    Your passkey is now registered ✅ — you can close that tab and return here. From now on you can sign in to any Microsoft service without a password.
⚠️ Tip: If you see "You can't add this sign-in method", your IT administrator may need to enable FIDO2 in the Entra tenant policy first.
